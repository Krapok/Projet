Presentation of the group.

We are 4 french undergraduate students of EPITA. We are asked to develop a game the year.

What is it about?

In this game, you control Wall-E, an highly trained robot and you have to prove you can complete the different levels you have to face. We created 5 levels to check if your abilities are good enough to handle the job you will have to do in the future.

* Interact with the environment!

* Try to complete the hardest levels with a second player!
